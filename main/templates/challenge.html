Strict Push-up Rules

  • Start in a full plank with locked elbows.
  • Lower until your chest clearly reaches the bottom position.
  • Press back to full lockout for every counted rep.
  • No extended resting in the top position.
  • No knee reps, half reps, or camera cuts during the set.

How To Record The Video

  • Use a side angle or diagonal angle that shows both depth and lockout.
  • Keep the whole set visible from start to finish.
  • Use normal speed, not slowed footage.
  • Upload to YouTube, TikTok, Google Drive, or another public link.
  • Test the link before submitting it so reviewers can open it immediately.

Verification Notes

The current system is manual on purpose. If your reps are unclear or questionable, your entry will not be ranked until it can be verified.

This protects the credibility of future rank-based products, plans, and public status.

Before You Submit

  • Open the video link in a private browser tab.
  • Make sure viewers do not need permission.
  • Check your final rep count once more.
  • Use the name you want the public to see.
