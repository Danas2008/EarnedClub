Rank framework

How ranks are defined

Ranks (tiers like Elite and Legend) are benchmarks used to contextualize placements on the leaderboard. They are reference points — the leaderboard itself remains the authoritative ranking.

Elite is treated as a top 10% style benchmark. Earned Legend is the top 1% style benchmark. These are standards athletes can aim for, but the leaderboard is where achievements are published.
