How The Challenge Works

  • Record one continuous set of strict push-ups from a visible angle.
  • Show your full body or at least your chest lockout and bottom position clearly.
  • Upload the video to YouTube, TikTok, Google Drive, or another public link.
  • Submit your score and link on the challenge page.
  • Your score appears on the open leaderboard immediately and shows as verified after manual review.
